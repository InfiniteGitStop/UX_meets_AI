# AI Career Roadmap for UI/UX Designers

A practical, high-paying transition guide for UI/UX designers who want to integrate AI, move into global roles, and future-proof their careers. This project provides a visual roadmap, step-by-step guidance, and key resources for designers aiming to specialize in AI-powered design roles.

---

## Features

- **Why AI + Design** – Explains the growing importance of AI knowledge in design roles.
- **Best AI Career Paths** – Highlights high-paying, global-ready roles such as AI Product Designer, Conversational UX Designer, and UX Engineer.
- **Tools to Learn** – Lists industry-standard AI tools, design & prototyping platforms, and optional engineering skills.
- **Courses That Matter** – Curated list of courses with high ROI for designers transitioning into AI.
- **Portfolio Transformation** – Guidance on AI-ifying existing projects to showcase relevant experience.
- **Salary Trajectory** – Comparison of roles and expected compensation in India and globally.
- **Interactive Cards** – Clickable cards reveal more detailed information for each section.

---

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5** – Structure of the site.
- **CSS3** – Styling, dark theme, responsive grid layout.
- **JavaScript** – Interactive card toggle functionality.

---

## How to Use

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open `index.html` in any web browser.
3. Click on the cards to expand and read detailed information about each topic.
